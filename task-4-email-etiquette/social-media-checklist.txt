✔️ Do’s
1)Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts
2)Think before posting (consider long-term impact)
3)Adjust privacy settings to control who sees your content
4)Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
5)Maintain a positive and respectful online presence


❌ Don’ts
1)Do not share personal/sensitive information (address, phone, passwords)
2)Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
3)Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
4)Avoid accepting friend/follow requests from unknown people
5)Do not overshare your daily activities or location publicly
